### 有什么区别

```html
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
<meta name="viewport" content="initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no"> 
```

```user-scalable=no```会禁止用户缩放

```width=device-width```让宽度等于设备宽度
